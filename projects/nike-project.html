Nike Mobile App Redesign

Problem Statement

Sneaker enthusiasts often face cluttered, inconsistent interfaces across multi-brand shopping apps, making exploration and comparison difficult. There's a clear need for a cleaner, more intuitive platform that brings multiple sneaker brands together under a single, premium experience.

Design Solution

To address this, I conceptualised a multi-brand sneaker app and chose to redesign the Discover screen by drawing inspiration from Nike's mobile app—known for its clean, minimal UI. The goal was to retain its simplicity while expanding functionality to support multi-brand-agnostic discovery.

Habit Loop App

Overview: Habit Loop is a mobile app that helps users create, track and maintain habits by using behaviour-driven nudges and progress visualisations.

Problem Statement

Users struggle to maintain consistent habits due to low motivation, lack of reminders, and unclear progress tracking, leading to drop-offs.

Design Solution

A habit-focused app that uses micro-goals, reminders, streaks and visual progress bars to keep users motivated and accountable.

Stakeholders: Users seeking behaviour change, product managers, growth teams

Target users: Learners, fitness enthusiasts, productivity seekers

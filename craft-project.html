Craft E-Commerce Website

Problem Statement

Gulabi Meenakari artists struggle with financial stability and reaching more customers because they lack good online presence or direct selling options. This makes it hard for them to fight against cheaper, machine-produced products.

Design Solution

The Gulabi Meenakari website empowers artisans by showcasing their craft and enabling direct-to-customer sales. With features like artisan profiles, e-commerce integration, and customer feedback, the platform boosts visibility, preserves heritage, and reduces dependency on middlemen.

Secondary Research

  • The secondary research began with studying research papers, articles/journals relevant to the topic.
  • Further steps involved the literature review which helped to understand the problem better & analyse on the basis of other people's work.

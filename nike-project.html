Nike Mobile App Redesign

Problem Statement

Sneaker enthusiasts often face cluttered, inconsistent interfaces across multi-brand shopping apps, making exploration and comparison difficult. There's a clear need for a cleaner, more intuitive platform that brings multiple sneaker brands together under a single, premium experience.

Design Solution

To address this, I conceptualised a multi-brand sneaker app and chose to redesign the Discover screen by drawing inspiration from Nike's mobile app—known for its clean, minimal UI. The goal was to retain its simplicity while expanding functionality to support multi-brand-agnostic discovery.

Secondary Research

  • The secondary research began with studying research papers, articles/journals relevant to the topic.
  • Further steps involved the literature review which helped to understand the problem better & analyse on the basis of other people's work.

Product Card UI Design

Overview

The product card for NIKE AIR Jordan 1 Spiderverse uses key social proof elements to build trust, trigger urgency, and boost conversions.

Key Social Proof Elements

1. Ratings & Reviews
  • 4.7 ★ from 4500 users
  • High rating and large volume build credibility and signal satisfaction.
  • Blue link draws attention and encourages exploration
2. Bestseller Badge
  • Red "Bestseller" tag highlights popularity.
  • Creates bandwagon effect—"everyone's buying it."
3. Sale Countdown
  • "Sale ends in 02:19:31" in red text.
  • Creates urgency and nudges quicker decisions.
